§ 01 Research

My work investigates neural and computational mechanisms underlying human cognition — approached with the rigour of a scientist and the patience of a craftsperson.

01
Predictive
Perception
How prior knowledge shapes what we see, hear, and feel — and when those predictions fail us in meaningful ways.
02
Memory
Consolidation
The slow process by which experience becomes knowledge. Sleep, time, and the gradual rewriting of what we remember.
03
Language
& Thought
Whether language shapes the concepts we can form — and what happens at the boundaries of the unspeakable.
04
Computational
Models
Building formal models of cognition precise enough to be proven wrong — and sometimes aren't.
